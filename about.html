🚚 Our Story

OUR
STORY

Taste on Wheels started with one simple belief: street food should be extraordinary. Not fast-food throwaway, not mediocre — but genuinely great food made with care, served with pride, right from a truck.

“Great food should be accessible to everyone — on every corner, every day.”

It started with a dream and a passion for food. We grew up eating incredible halal food at home — rich with spices, layered flavors and cooked with love. We couldn’t understand why those flavors never made it to the streets.

So we built Taste on Wheels. We took everything we loved about great Middle Eastern and street food cooking — the lamb over rice, the perfectly spiced wraps, the handcrafted gyro — and brought it to where people actually are.

From our very first day on the road, the response blew us away. People lined up. They came back. They brought their families. They tagged us on Instagram. And slowly, Taste on Wheels became more than a truck — it became a community.

Today we serve hundreds of people every week. We’ve evolved our menu, added signature items like our BYOB chips experience, and kept one promise the entire time: every plate that leaves our window is made fresh, made with care, and made to satisfy.

Every item on our menu is 100% Halal certified. We believe in transparency — our meat is sourced locally, halal-certified and prepared fresh daily. No shortcuts, no compromise.
