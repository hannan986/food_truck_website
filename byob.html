BYOB

Bring Your Own Bag of Chips

Bring any bag of your favourite chips and we’ll fill it with your choice of seasoned meat, melted cheese and all our signature fixings. The most unique street food experience you’ve never tried — until now.

The Process

HOW IT WORKS

🛍️
BRING YOUR BAG

Grab your favourite bag of chips — Doritos, Lay’s, Takis, Cheetos, or any other brand. Sealed bags preferred.

🥩
CHOOSE YOUR PROTEIN

Pick from our seasoned chicken, beef, lamb, gyro meat or crispy falafel. All halal, all made fresh to order.

🧀
WE FILL IT UP

We open the bag and fill it with your chosen protein, melted cheese, jalapeños and all our special fixings. Then we seal it and hand it back!

Pick Your Protein

CHOOSE YOUR FILLING

🍗
Chicken / Beef

Juicy seasoned chicken or halal beef — savory, smoky and satisfying. The crowd pleaser.

🍖

Our signature seasoned lamb or authentic gyro meat — bold, rich and full of flavor. The fan favourite.

🧆
Falafel

Crispy golden falafel made fresh daily — hearty, flavorful and fully vegetarian. Pairs perfectly with our tahini.
